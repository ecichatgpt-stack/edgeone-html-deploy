Expected Outcome: Technical foundation ready, keyword target list finalized, +5-10 daily visits from quick wins. 预期结果：技术基础就绪，关键词目标列表确定，快速胜利带来每日+5-10次访问。

MONTHS 3-4第3-4个月

📝 Content Development Phase 1📝 内容开发第一阶段

MONTHS 5-6第5-6个月

🚀 Baidu Acceleration🚀 百度加速

Expected Outcome: Baidu traffic reaches 30-50 daily, total keyword count 40-50, +80-120 daily visits total. 预期结果：百度流量达到每日30-50次，总关键词数量40-50个，总计每日+80-120次访问。

MONTHS 7-9第7-9个月

📈 Scaling & Optimization📈 扩展与优化

Competitive Index Methodology 竞争指数计算方法

The Competitive Index is calculated using a weighted formula: (Total Traffic × 0.5) + (Total Keywords × 0.3) + (Top 20 Keywords × 0.2), normalized to a 0-100 scale. This provides a holistic view of each company's SEO market position, combining traffic volume, keyword diversity, and high-value ranking distribution. 竞争指数使用加权公式计算：(总流量 × 0.5) + (关键词总数 × 0.3) + (前20名关键词 × 0.2)，归一化至0-100分值范围。这提供了每家公司SEO市场地位的整体视图，结合了流量规模、关键词多样性和高价值排名分布。
